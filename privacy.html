Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

CamSecurity is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our contact or enquiry form. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Your message or service requirement

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your service enquiry or quote request
  • To schedule a free site survey or consultation
  • To provide installation or maintenance services
  • To send relevant follow-ups regarding your enquiry

We do not use your data for marketing, promotions, or newsletters without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is only accessible to our internal team members who need it to fulfil your service request.

5. Data Security

We take your data security seriously. Our contact form is processed securely via Formspree, a trusted third-party service. No personal data is stored on our web server. All data is handled using industry-standard security practices.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use any tracking cookies, analytics tools, or third-party advertising scripts. We do not monitor your browsing behaviour or collect any data without your knowledge.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn. Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only for as long as necessary to fulfil your service request or resolve any related queries. Once no longer needed, your data is deleted from our records.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for any future communication

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
